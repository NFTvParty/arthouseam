About the Show

A new mixtape show done the old way to start your weekend in NYC

ArthouseAM airs Fridays at 9pm on WPAT 930 AM in NYC — a weekly ephemeral vinyl mixtape celebrating the multicultural, multigenerational power of dance music. Free for anyone in NYC with a radio. No algorithms, no payola, no AI, and no replays online.

ArthouseAM does not engage in social media. Listeners can connect 24/7 through a toll free “no request line” with updates and announcements. While we don’t take requests, we want to hear from you: favorite NYC clubs and tracks (old and new), where you’re listening from, and DJs we should have on the air in the future.

Call NOW: 1-888-844-2505

Hosted by Stottle (aka Stottleplex), returning to radio after over three decades, alongside NYC DJ Raw Q — both spinning monthly sets and featuring guest DJs.

  • April 3 — Stottle of Spring Valley Polo Club Records
  • April 10 — Raw Q
  • April 17 — TBA (Raw Q guest)
  • April 24 — CB Smoove of House of Frequencies Records (Stottle guest)

Each guest delivers a 90-minute mixtape they’d play if no one else existed but them and the dance floor. We will post the complete tracklist and backstory here in blog form, as well as the opportunity to purchase a cassette copy of each mix, limited to 45 per show. (Proceeds from all cassette sales are split with the DJ and go directly to keeping the show on the air.)
