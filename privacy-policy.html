Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 2026 — ArthouseAM / Truly Untitled LLC

Overview

ArthouseAM is a weekly radio program broadcast on WPAT 930 AM, New York. This policy describes how we handle information through our website (arthouseam.com) and our toll-free listener hotline.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information through this website. We do not use tracking cookies, analytics, or advertising technologies. Our toll-free listener line accepts inbound calls and text messages voluntarily initiated by listeners. We do not collect names, email addresses, or payment information.

Telephone & Hotline

Our toll-free number (+1 888 844 2505) is an inbound listener line. Listeners call or text voluntarily to submit song requests, shoutouts, and feedback during or after broadcasts. The number is announced on-air during each program. No unsolicited outbound calls or marketing messages are transmitted.

SMS Text Messaging

Listeners may send text messages (SMS) to our toll-free number to submit song requests, shoutouts, and feedback. By texting this number, you consent to receive occasional automated replies related to the ArthouseAM program. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. Reply STOP at any time to opt out of SMS messages. Reply HELP for assistance. We do not sell or share your phone number with third parties for marketing purposes.

Opt-In & Opt-Out

SMS opt-in occurs when a listener initiates contact by texting our toll-free number. No prior registration is required. To opt out at any time, reply STOP to any message from our number. You will receive one final confirmation and no further messages will be sent.

Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics services.

Third-Party Services

Voice services are provided by Twilio Inc. For information on Twilio’s data practices, visit twilio.com/legal/privacy.

Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. The effective date above reflects the most recent revision.
