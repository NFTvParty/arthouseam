Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 2026 — ArthouseAM / Truly Untitled LLC

Overview

ArthouseAM is a weekly radio program broadcast on WPAT 930 AM, New York. This policy describes how we handle information through our website (arthouseam.com) and our toll-free listener hotline.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information through this website. We do not use tracking cookies, analytics, or advertising technologies. Our toll-free hotline is an inbound-only informational line — callers may voluntarily leave a voice message. We do not collect names, email addresses, or payment information.

Telephone & Hotline

Our toll-free number is an inbound listener hotline. No outbound calls or SMS messages are sent. No automated marketing messages are transmitted. Callers dial in voluntarily to hear program information and announcements. The act of calling constitutes consent to hear a recorded message.

Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics services.

Third-Party Services

Voice services are provided by Twilio Inc. For information on Twilio’s data practices, visit twilio.com/legal/privacy.

Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. The effective date above reflects the most recent revision.
